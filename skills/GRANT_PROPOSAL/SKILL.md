# Grant Proposal Research

## Profile
- **Duration:** Proposal writing: 1-3 months. Project if funded: 1-3 years
- **Compute:** Justify in budget (can request anything if well-argued)
- **Budget:** Define and justify (typical: $10K-500K depending on funder)
- **Scope:** Must be ambitious enough to justify funding, feasible enough to be credible

## Constraints
- Must align with funder's priorities and call themes
- Must demonstrate team capability (preliminary results help)
- Must include clear milestones and deliverables
- Budget must be justified line-by-line
- Impact statement required (societal, economic, or scientific)
- Risk mitigation plan needed

## Proposal Structure
1. **Executive Summary** (1 page)
2. **Problem Statement** (why this matters NOW)
3. **Literature Gap** (what's missing, why existing solutions fail)
4. **Proposed Approach** (methodology, innovation, feasibility)
5. **Preliminary Results** (proof that you can do this)
6. **Timeline & Milestones** (Gantt chart, deliverables per quarter)
7. **Budget Justification** (personnel, equipment, travel, overhead)
8. **Team & Expertise** (why YOU should do this)
9. **Impact & Dissemination** (publications, patents, societal benefit)
10. **Risk Assessment** (what could go wrong, plan B)

## Quality Bar
- Novelty: must advance the field, not just apply existing methods
- Feasibility: reviewer must believe you can deliver in the timeline
- Impact: clear beneficiaries (patients, industry, society, science)
- Team: demonstrated track record in the area
- Budget: realistic, not inflated, well-justified

## Output Expectations
- Full proposal document (10-30 pages depending on funder)
- Budget spreadsheet
- CV/biosketch of PI and co-PIs
- Letters of support (if required)
- Data management plan
- Ethics statement

## Common Funders
- National: NSF, NIH, ERC, JSPS, DIKTI/Kemendikbud
- International: Gates Foundation, Wellcome Trust, WHO
- Industry: Google Research, Microsoft Research, Meta
- Regional: ASEAN grants, bilateral cooperation programs
