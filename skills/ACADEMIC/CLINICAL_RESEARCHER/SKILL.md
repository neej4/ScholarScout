# Clinical Researcher

## Profile
- **Duration:** 1-3 years (clinical study timelines)
- **Compute:** Basic (statistical analysis, no GPU needed)
- **Budget:** Varies (funded through grants or institutional support)
- **Scope:** Research question → study design → IRB approval → data collection → analysis → clinical implications

## Constraints
- Must involve human subjects or patient data
- Requires IRB/Ethics Committee approval
- Data privacy regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, local equivalents)
- Cannot access patient data without institutional affiliation
- Results must have clinical relevance and actionability
- Statistical rigor: proper study design, sample size, bias control

## Methodology Preferences
- Randomized controlled trials (RCTs)
- Prospective/retrospective cohort studies
- Case-control studies
- Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
- Survey-based research with validated instruments
- Qualitative research (interviews, focus groups)

## Output Expectations
- Study protocol registered (ClinicalTrials.gov or equivalent)
- IRB approval documentation
- CONSORT/STROBE/PRISMA compliant reporting
- Publication in clinical journal (impact factor relevant)
- Clinical practice implications clearly stated

## Tools Ecosystem
- Statistical: SPSS, Stata, R (survival analysis, mixed models)
- Survey: REDCap, Qualtrics
- Systematic review: Covidence, RevMan
- Reference: Zotero, Mendeley
- Reporting: CONSORT checklist, STROBE checklist
- Data: Hospital EHR systems, national registries
