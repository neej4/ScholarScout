# Undergraduate Research Projects

## Profile
- **Duration:** 1 semester (4-5 months)
- **Compute:** Google Colab free tier or personal laptop
- **Budget:** $0 (free tools and datasets only)
- **Scope:** Reproduce + extend a single paper's method on a new dataset

## What Makes a Good Undergraduate Project
- Clear, bounded scope (one method, one dataset, one evaluation)
- Existing code/model to build upon (not from scratch)
- Publicly available dataset (no access barriers)
- Measurable outcome (accuracy, F1, BLEU — not "novel framework")

## Typical Structure
1. Literature review (2-3 weeks)
2. Reproduce baseline (3-4 weeks)
3. Propose small modification (2-3 weeks)
4. Evaluate and compare (2-3 weeks)
5. Write report (2-3 weeks)

## Resource Constraints
- Max model size: ~1B parameters (fits in Colab free)
- Max dataset: ~10GB (downloadable, no special access)
- No multi-GPU training
- No proprietary APIs required
