Welcome to ScholarScout

From papers to ideas — research or product, your choice.

1. Select Categories

Choose research fields from the left panel. 80+ categories across 10 disciplines.

2. Set Your Context

Click "Context" to describe your background. Choose your research approach (AI, Lab, Clinical, etc).

3. Run Pipeline

Click "Run Pipeline" to fetch papers, analyze trends, and generate tailored research ideas.

4. Explore & Bookmark

Click "Show more" for full details. Bookmark favorites. Use "Deep Dive" for methodology analysis.
